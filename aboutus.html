Our Story.

From pickup to delivery, Washly brings fast, reliable, and high quality laundry service right to your door so you never worry about your clothes again.

D
Devesh
Founder, Washly

Washly was founded by Devesh, a young entrepreneur who identified a common urban problem lack of time for everyday essentials like laundry.

Living in a fast-paced city like Gurgaon, Devesh realized that people were constantly juggling work, studies, and personal life, leaving little time for routine chores. Traditional laundry services were slow, inconsistent, or just plain inconvenient.

This sparked the idea to create a tech-enabled laundry service that is quick, reliable, and customer-focused. What started as a simple idea evolved into Washly a platform designed for seamless pickup, professional cleaning, and fast doorstep delivery.

🎯 Our vision

Devesh aims to build Washly into a leading quick-commerce laundry brand in India, combining technology, logistics, and premium service standards.

The goal is not just to clean clothes, but to save time, simplify lives, and create a dependable service people can trust every single day.
